CS: GO on the occasion of IEEE day 2017

To relax and chill students mood after Mid-term Exams, On the occasion of IEEE Day Celebration, IEEE Student Branch in collaboration with HP Omen brought India College Gaming Championship. This event was free and open to all. Details of Championship are as followed: - Tech Showcasing: We had about 5-6 HP Omen Laptops for the students to see and try games out on. - ESL Qualifier: We hosted a CS: GO tournament exclusively for the students of LNMIIT where the top five individuals will represent LNMIIT in the ESL College League. Additionally, we didn't charge the students for taking part in this tournament. The tournament will be held on HP Omen laptops for the premium gaming experience. - VR Zone: Along with the tournament, students also had a VR zone for people to enjoy. Get to experience some of the best VR games on the HTC Vive. We had some in-game challenges where people won Goodie Bags containing branded merchandise.
