GENERAL QUIZ

Tags: LNMIIT, Student branch

LNMIIT IEEE Student Branch along with Quiz Club organised a general quiz...

LNMIIT IEEE Student Branch organised the General Quiz in collaboration with The Quiz Club on the occasion of IEEE day. There was a huge participation from students of all years, ready to test their knowledge of the general world.IEEE Day stickers and posters were also distributed among the participants. We also celebrated IEEE Day by cake cutting ceremony with our branch counselor; Dr. Soumitra Debnath. The Quiz was structured to contain 2 rounds. First Round was the question answer round containing 25 questions. Each question was displayed on the screen for 15 seconds. Second round was the tie breaker round, in case of any ties.

Results

The first position was jointly held by two teams: Rachit Sharma, Shikhar Dalela, Shankar Krishnan, Kartik Shankhavaramand Rahul Dugar, Anuj Varshney The second position was held by: Daksh Balyan, Harshit Garg and Yash Maheshwari.
