LNMHacks

LNMHacks is a one-stop platform that brings together developers, designers and thinkers. It encourages the idea of converting into reality the cherry-picked ideas from the unstoppable and random stream of thoughts in mind. Making things, breaking conventions and re-working upon them with an innovative mind is what has produced the best of products. Carrying forward the legacy of LNMHacks, the second edition LNMHacks 2.0 was successfully hosted at The LNM Institute of Information Technology from 11th November 2017 to 12th November 2017. Receiving an overwhelming response from 700+ participants in Elimination Round. The main event saw 80+ teams consisting of 250 participants at the on-site hackathon.

Pre Event 1

To make the newbies acquainted with the hackathon culture, there were two pre-events well executed before the main event. In Pre-event 1, Mr. Osho Parth, a cybersecurity expert and Mozilla Regional Repo addressed students regarding tracks of LNMHacks 2.0 and an idea of how to approach hackathons and benefits of participation in Open-Source

Pre Event 2

Pre-Event 2 saw a great interactive session with Greg Price and Andrew Drake. They discussed the emerging trends in programming, the versatility of JavaScript and Python, the importance of hackathons and also shared a whole lot of experiences with the enthusiastic audience. Greg's Profile : http://web.mit.edu/price/ Greg has worked at top tech companies in the valley Including Quora and Dropbox. He has received his master's degree from MIT in 2008, and his bachelor's in mathematics from Harvard in 2007. Andrew's profile: https://adrake.org/ He is a software engineer at Dropbox working on server infrastructure having previously worked on the JavaScript engine at Mozilla. He also has a degree in CS from CMU, Pittsburg.

Team

LNMHacks was organized by IEEE Student Branch and IEEE WIE LNMIIT. The organising committee under the guidance of Dr. Subrat K Dash, HOD, Dept. of Computer Science as faculty mentor, headed by Vivek Kumar consisted of Vishal Sanghai, Gourav Khatri, Neelesh Singh Rajpurohit, Gaurav Kulkarni, Divyanshu Rawat, Siddharth Garg, Shubham Mangal and Nikhil Paliwal. All of them gave tireless inputs to pull-off LNMHacks 2.0.

Inaugural Ceremony

The Inaugural Ceremony was conducted on the morning of 11th November 2017. Incoming Participants, The Director, LNMIIT, Professors at LNMIIT , organising committee members along with the Judges Mr. Devanshu Shukla (Founder, HackVeda), Prof. Narasimha Bolloju (CSE Dept. LNMIIT) adorned the ceremony. Mr. Abhishek Rathore (Co-Founder, YouStart Labs) mentored the Organising Committee in carrying the proceedings for the event. With the spirit of Code All Night. Eat All Night, the organisers of LNMHacks 2.0 did not let the coding spirit of participants touch any low. There were multiple fun-tech quizzes conducted live on Twitter post-midnight, keeping the spirits high. The quiz was accompanied by many sponsored swags for all the participants. Further some amazing food was made available in the middle of the night, so that the participants could concentrate better on their hack.

Day 2

Pre-Evaluation was conducted in the morning session. It aimed at helping maximum teams to complete their projects in stipulated time. On the same day, at 12 noon the portal of HackerEarth was shut down for submission. With the guidance of judges and mentors, 11 teams were shortlisted for the final round.

Final Round and Results

In the final round, teams needed to prepare a short and precise presentation about the problem statement of their product and give its demonstration. Mr. Devanshu Shukla, Mr. Dilnawaz Khan and Prof. Narsimha Bolloju accepted the responsibility of judging the last round. This round saw some quality innovations and judges were glad to interact with the young minds. Winners of the LNMHacks 2.0 were declared. Below is a list of them: 1st: akshatsma - LNMIIT “ Human computer vision | Mini Jarvis under theme Computer Vision.” Members: 1)Akshat Sharma 2)Animesh Gupta 2nd: Team Rocket - JECRC University “ IoT And Automation For Dif erently Abled under the theme IoT” Members: 1) Mohit Joher 2)Vipul Lalwani 3)Bhavya Choubisa 4)Ayush Chauhan 3rd: Techkies - LNMIIT “ Optio under theme General ” Members: 1) Vishwesh Jainkuniya 2) Khushboo Baheti 3) Rahul Dugar At the end, Mr. Shukla gave an insight of how one should approach hackathons, made students aware of some amazing resources on the internet and wished LNMHacks 3.0 to be even bigger and better.
